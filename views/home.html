Let's take a look at each element or part of the story (plot).

Exposition is simply the beginning of the story - what action was taken to start a series of actions. Let me add here that many believe that this would include the set up of a story or the elements of character and setting are these are very important components of a story but we are talking about more of the plot movement or in other words - the actions that take place moving a character to a point of change and returning that character as having changed a thought, a behavior, or merely an action.
